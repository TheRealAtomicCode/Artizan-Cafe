About us

The Artizan Cafe is one of the top 40 restaurants in the UK, conveniently located in downtown Manchester. Come to a perfect place for a delicious breakfast, perhaps for a cup of cappuccino to lift your mood, or for a great lunch after your hard working day. For four decades, Artizan Cafe has been serving the local community with the world's famous cuisines. Our diversified menu offers a wide selection of meals for any taste. Visit us to feel the warmth of our hospitality and to enjoy your favorite cuisine!
